Wendy didn't want to go to school today, saying she was tired. Kham reminded her about her birthday party tomorrow and she suddenly found energy. She got to take her bicycle to school yesterday. Tomorrow I get to see her dance at school, then she will have a birthday party here at the house. I will sleep at a hotel Friday and Saturday nights. If it's warm enough, the girls will come swim on Saturday, but it's been rather cold lately. Sunday I fly to Chiang Mai. Monday Wendy has a birthday party at school.
