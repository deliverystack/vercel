It's nice to have the pins out of my foot. It's still swollen and it hurts a bit, but I can walk with a minor limp. I think I can swim in a few days. I don't know when I will be able to ride a bicycle or a motorcycle again, but at least I can drive a car. I fly to Chiang Mai Sunday, which I would not have wanted to do with a cast and crutches.

Songs:

  • De La Soul: The Magic Number: 3 Feet High and Rising is a great album.

It seems like I'm in a bit of a dry spell for creating content. To be honest, I think I drank too much coffee this morning; I'm a bit faint, like I need to eat more protein or something. I should probably try to meditate or listen to music or watch something brainless, but it's increasingly hard for me to focus on such things. Instead, I'll try to do some of the TODOs that I always leave for myself.

It's 12:00 and I think I figured it out. I don't really want to sit at the computer anymore today, but I can't do much else. I may try reading.
