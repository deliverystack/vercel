I was listening to the following video last night but got upset at about 7:10 when the voice of Alan Watts, who died in 1973 (the year I was born), mentioned texts and then started talking about himself in the third person. It doesn't make the content worthless, but AI pretending to be someone bothers me. Afterwards, I noticed that the voice was rather flat and the audio quality might have been too good to have been recorded during his lifetime. I think I fell asleep after 10:00 last night and got up around 4:00 this morning.
