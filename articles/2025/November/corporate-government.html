Should Governments Be Run Like Corporations?

//TODO: trumpteam image TL;DR: Apply Betteridge's law of headlines; the answer is no.

About a year ago, I participated in the following LinkedIn thread that ended up with over 7,000 comments:

https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn%3Ali%3Aactivity%3A7269737472706703360

If I was a betting man, I'd back these guys to run my country.

There was a debate recently saying trump, Elon and his advisors are not the right people because a country is not a business.......And I couldn't disagree more.

A country has a GDP ( an income stream target per year) like a company has a revenue target.

A country has a population it needs to grow, keep happy and thrive. Like a company has employees.

A country has prospects and new opportunities and threats it needs to navigate, like a company faces.

The list could go on but these guys are great leaders, and they have ran corporations, businesses and crafted thriving eco systems within their business spheres. They know their stuff.

This is the model the UK and other countries should follow, bring in credible business leaders to lead the country they are in, not those who have a degree on paper.
